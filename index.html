About Me

I am an engineer with expertise in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. I received my MSc degree from the University of Bristol in 2016 and I have been working in the industry since. I am interested in autonomous systems and AI with emphasis on perception, decision making and control.

I have started a PhD at City, University of London in 2019. I am researching abstract state representations for reinforcement learning (e.g. using objects and their relationships) to improve long term planning.

I enjoy being a research engineer, working on cutting edge technologies and developing systems based on the latest research results to solve real life problems. Besides focusing on the technical challenges, I also find the ethics of technology crucial for making the right decisions as we progress further as a society.

For my education and employment history please see my CV or connect with me on LinkedIn.
